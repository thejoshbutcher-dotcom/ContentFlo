How it works

One flow. Start to finish.

Idea→ Script→ Record→ Edit→ Schedule→ Publish→ Track
STEP 1

Set up your brand

Tell CreatorFlo your niche, audience, topics, and content buckets once. Every board, template, and brainstorm is built from your profile — and you can run multiple brands side by side.

STEP 2

Brainstorm & script

Stack the deck until an idea clicks, add it to your pipeline, and open the card to a ready-made script template with hooks, frameworks, and CTAs for that exact format.

STEP 3

Drag it to done

Move cards through scripting, filming, and editing, then drop them on the calendar. Open the app any morning and know exactly what today is for.
